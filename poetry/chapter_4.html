Twisted Fictions

Chilled To The Bones

Laying beside you
You feel cold to the touch
I tried to warm you up
But it only turned you into ashes...

The Ring

A family heirloom for generations
Stolen and hidden away
I had to get it back, no matter what
It was a mission I had to finish
At last, the ring is on the palm of my hands
Right after I've cut the last layer of his stomach
Dug in deep to get it back...
I'll never lose this ring again.

Perfect Smile

A smile was carved on my face
As I came out of the room and saw you
Tears, running down my cheeks
You were happy to see me too
I just hope you notice that this smile is carved permanently...

Eternal Friendship

A dog is a man's best friend
So when the apocalypse dawned upon us
I gave my all to keep my best friend alive
Even if it means that I'll be torn to pieces, bit by bit..
Just so she won't starve.

Stare

I've been admiring you for so long
I enjoy watching you every second of the day
It's too bad that I can only stare through a hole
As I'm only living inside your walls.

Eyes

Her eyes were the most beautiful I've seen
Deep, blue, oceanic eyes
That stared at me intently
The windows to her soul...
I loved it so much
So I took it from her.
