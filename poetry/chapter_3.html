CHAPTER III: The Void

Talon

Sa bawat pagaspas ng hangin,
Sa layo ng nararating ng tingin,
Ang mabilis na pag agos ng tubig,
Malakas ngunit marahan sa pandinig.

Bawat patak ng tubig na sya ring pagpatak ng oras,
Sa bawat segundong lumilipas,
Matatamis na alaala'y naglalaro't kumukumpas,
Kasabay ng pagsayaw ng tubig at mga rosas.

Di mapigilang mapangiti sa mga alala,
Mga larawang gumuguhit sa isip tuwina.
Mga panahong namumuhay ng ikaw ang kasama,
O kay sarap balikan sa aking memorya.

Kasabay ng malakas na alon ang pagpatak ng luha,
Ang paglawak ng ngiti't pagkislap ng mga mata.
Paghinga ng malalim at ang mahinang pagtawa,
Habang sinasambit "Hintayin mo ko mahal ko, tayo'y muling magkikita."

Ang pagbibitiw ng salita kasabay ng paglundag ng mga paa,
Ang pagtalon sa talon kung san ka namayapa,
Pipiliin kang makasama, kahit sa kabilang buhay pa,
O aking sinta, ako'y paparating na.

A Tunnel Leading to Nowhere

This light bulb lasted for quite some time
But it has served its purpose
As it began to flicker, a steady beat
Striving to serve light for one more second..

As I looked around this empty room
A mirror in front, flashing with the bulb
Walking closer to the reflection I see
flicker... a kid with a carved smile
flicker... a glimpse of a teen's first kiss
flicker.. floods of tears wrecked the room along with the pieces of a heart scattered on the floor
flicker... graduation day finally came

A smile appeared along my face
Not until it all went dark..
The bulb, slowly giving up

Another
flicker..a broken image of an alcoholic
flicker..smashed bottles, bleeding hands
flicker.. a person's face, tho it's distorted
flicker.. coming closer and closer
flicker.. growing more deformed
flicker.. a twisted expression was written
flicker.. on a face so familiar
flicker.. a vision of..me

I switched the lights off
As I stared blankly at the dark figure
An unknown reflection stands before me

Shivering from what I saw
Takes a step back, ready to leave
Leaving this empty room behind
Along with all the memories, stored in this mirror
Hoping the next owner won't notice
This dirty mirror, stained with a dark past

Underneath the Moon

A thousand thoughts
From this brain of mine
Struggles to sleep
Can't tell the time

The moonlight shines
Upon my bed
The ambiance shows
The night is dead

No stars are seen
Nothing is present
Except the moon
As it descend

The moon lights up
This big dark dome
Along with my mind
The more it roams

Time slows down
The more I look
At this big moon
My breath, it took

I try again
To close my eyes
With the help of the moon
As I continue to lie

In hopes of seeing
The morning sun
The moment I wake up
After the night is done

If It Didn't Happen

What if the sun never shone
Will my glimmer ever be seen?
Will your eyes light up
The way that it had been

What if the flowers never bloomed
With such a soothing fragrance
The way your love blossomed
And created wonderful romance

What if the rain didn't pour
The way it did that night
The moment that we met
Calming, with a moment of fright

What if the breeze changed
And altered the atmosphere
Giving us cold shoulders
Our courage, replaced with fear

What if my eyes didn't lay on yours
Would we ever be able to look the same
Deeply at one anothers' eye
Then bewildered, now tamed

What if it didn't happen
Time freezing the moment we met
Surrounded by nothing but silence
As a smile appeared, carved and set

The biggest what if in my life
Would always remain a what if
For no matter how many times I retry
I'll always pick you and you again.
